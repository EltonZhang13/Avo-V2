The Juicy Details

The truth about your “orange juice,” is that it’s not real juice, even the 100% types. Fruits are seasonal. Even though they can still be bought year‐round, it doesn’t mean they’re going to taste good year‐round. Fruits are squeezed for their juices, which are then stored in huge tanks, where they keep for a year. This process makes the juice lose all it’s flavor, so ethyl butyrate is added, or as I like to call it, “Magical Packets of Flavor.” While it hasn’t been linked to health dangers, yet, there's no reason it should be in your juice. Perhaps the most important thing for you to remeber is to drink real juice. Fruit juice, unless it was literally squeezed in front of your eyes, is not going to be healthy for you. Processed fruit juice is just another way for you to load up on sugar, without any of the useful nutrients.
